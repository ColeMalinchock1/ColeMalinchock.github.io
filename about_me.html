Things I Do!

I love to bike pack! In 2020, I was able to bike across the United States camping every night under the stars to raise money for the Be Loud! Sophie Foundation. I was lucky enough to do it up the entire East Coast in 2021. And today, I continue to enjoy my passion as I race collegiately for NC State. Click here to learn more about the bike trips!


I competed in FIRST Robotics Competitions with Team 4829, The Titanium Tigers, in high school. I was able to work with an amazing team to build a robot in 2022 that won our schools first Regional Championship for FRC and competed in the Worlds Championship in Houston! Click here to learn more about the Titanium Tigers!


I've been backpacking all over the country, and even in Iceland! Being able to get outside and go backpacking has shown me how important it is to respect and take care of nature. If I ever need to relax and calm my mind, I'll find a place outside.
