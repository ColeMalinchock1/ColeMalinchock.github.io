Project of the Month: Autonomous Aquatic Vessel

The Autonomous Aquatic Vessel or AAV is a multi-generational project with the goal of developing a vessel that is capable of developing a route through a body of water and collecting data on the water. This personal project was funded by a Park Enrichment Grant and is my first attempt at developing a robotic system in a natural environment.
